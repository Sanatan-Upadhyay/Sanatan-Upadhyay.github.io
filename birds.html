Seabirds

Oceans aren’t just for fish.
Seabirds depend on healthy oceans for food and much more.
From the emperor penguin to the blue-footed booby to the brown pelican,
seabirds come in all shapes and sizes (some can’t even fly) and
play a vital role in ocean ecosystems.
